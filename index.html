what we do

INTERNET AND YOUR PRIVACY

The Internet is a gateway to the tapestry of collective human knowledge. It is what connects us, informs us and educates us.

It is where the majority of public discourse happens, where we talk to our loved ones who are away from us, where we get the latest news, where we shop. Frankly, it touches and affects every aspect of human experience.

WE THINK OUT OF THE BOX

We are developing a portable device that will anonymise and mask all web traffic and hide your location while you are connected to Wi-Fi network.

Our solution protects any device that can connect to wireless networks and hides activities on that device from prying eyes.

Our solution secures your internet connection at any open public wifi network or private network, letting you surf the web without worry.

Who We are

We are a team of engineers, project managers, security professionals and privacy aware individuals that lived in many different countries on the planet.
